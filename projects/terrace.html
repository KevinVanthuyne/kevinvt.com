_Terrace

Apr - Jun 2019

For several years Jeugdhuis Tronk put some sofa's outside of the youth house during the summer to provide a makeshift terrace to customers. This wasn't very practical since they had to be moved in and out every evening during opening hours and it wasn't very cosy.

After the summer of 2018 though, we got permission from the town to install a permanent terrace. I started modelling a design in Fusion 360 and composed a list of the necessary materials. With the help of fellow board members we constructed the terrace according to the plans. Afterwards we painted it and made several benches from recycled pallets. The only thing left to do is add some plants to the fence to make it look a bit nicer.
